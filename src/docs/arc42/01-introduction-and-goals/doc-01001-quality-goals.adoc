== [[quality-goals]]Quality Goals

Only the top architecture-driving quality objectives are listed here. Chapter
xref:quality-requirements[] refines them into measurable draft scenarios and
also captures derived quality requirements.

[cols="1,2,3,2", options="header"]
|===
| ID | Goal | Meaning | Approach

| QG-001
| Reproducibility
| Same committed inputs produce the same validation and generated-documentation
outputs.
| xref:solution-strategy[Deterministic validation and generation]

| QG-002
| Reviewability
| Architecture knowledge changes are small, explicit, traceable, and suitable
for pull-request review.
| xref:solution-strategy[Source-first artifact model]

| QG-003
| Traceability
| Architecture relationships are modeled with stable IDs and explicit
metadata, not inferred from prose alone.
| xref:solution-strategy[Metadata-driven traceability]

| QG-004
| Content reuse
| Shared statements, definitions, and constraints are authored once as source
fragments and included where needed.
| xref:solution-strategy[Reusable source fragments]

| QG-005
| Portability
| Core skills and architecture rules remain useful across AI engines and
documentation publication paths.
| xref:solution-strategy[Engine-independent core]
|===
