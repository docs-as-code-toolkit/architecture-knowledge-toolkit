= R-006: Adapter Boundaries Remain Unclear

== Status

Proposed.

This risk is AI-assisted. It identifies a concern for review and does not
decide risk acceptance.

== Risk Statement

Because differences between AI engines are not yet well understood,
runtime-specific guidance may be placed inconsistently, leading to coupling in
core skills or duplicated adapter documentation.

== Assessment

[cols="1,3", options="header"]
|===
| Field | Value
| Likelihood | Medium.
| Impact | Medium.
| Priority | Medium.
| Timeframe | Near-term.
| Confidence | Medium.
|===

== Mitigation Options

* Keep runtime details under `adapters/` until Q-ARCH-006 is resolved.
* Review runtime-specific changes against xref:qs-005-adapter-boundary[].
* Add concrete examples when engine differences become known.

== Decision or Acceptance

The risk remains under analysis until the accountable owner confirms the
assessment and mitigation path.
