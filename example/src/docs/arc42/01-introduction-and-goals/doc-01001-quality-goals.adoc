[[quality-goals]]
== Quality Goals

[cols="1,2,3", options="header"]
|===
| ID | Goal | Rationale

| QG-001
| Privacy
| Personal spending data must remain protected from unauthorized access.

| QG-002
| Data integrity
| Budget totals must not silently lose or duplicate transactions.

| QG-003
| Offline resilience
| Mobile users should continue entering expenses when connectivity is poor.

| QG-004
| Usability
| Recording a daily expense must be fast enough to become a habit.
|===
